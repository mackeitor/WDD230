Audience

The target audience for the site are business people who want to expand their business and also want to be included in government programs. We can divide our target in two main categories. The first one will be entepreneurs that have experience and are willing to help new entepreneurs. And the second will be the new entepreneurs that are looking for support, experiece, and representation.

Scenarios

  1. A business person who is seeking for funding.
  2. An entepreneur that is looking for marketing.
  3. A business person who wants credibility in his area.
  4. A business person who is looking for partnership.
  5. A business person who is looking for exclusive advertising perks.

Personas

  1. Isaac Fuentes is a young entrepreneur who has decided to start a food business. He is looking to reach more people with advertising and network management. Perhaps he will look for a partnership so he can have the support of someone with experience.
  2. Adriana Tornar is a French woman who recently moved to Mexico and decided to start a crepe business. She is looking for people with experience to be able to grow in the business field.
